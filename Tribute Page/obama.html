Barack Hussein Obama II

44th U.S. President

President Obama, Served from 2009-2017

Obama's Timeline:

  • 1961- born on August 4, 1961 in Honolulu, Hawaii
  • 1979- attends Occidental College from a full scholarship and transfers to Columbia University in 1983 graduating with a political science degree
  • 1980- moves to Chicago as director of the Developing Communities Project
  • 1988- attends Harvard law school and meets his wife, Michelle Robinson, at the Chicago law firm of Sidley Austin
  • 1991- teaches constitutional law at the University of Chicago Law School
  • 1994- director of the Woods Fund of Chicago (Developing Communities Project)
  • 1996- elected to the Illinois State Senate
  • 2005- elected as the U.S. Senator from Illinois
  • 2008- elected President of the United States beating Hillary Clinton within the Democratic Party presidential primaries and John McCain representing the Republican Party.
  • Major Policies passed in office: Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (Obamacare) and The American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 to serve as an economic stimulus for the Great Recession
  • 2012- relected President defeating Republican Mitt Romney
  • Major Policies passed in office: legalized same-sex marriage federally and increased gun control policies from mass school shootings
  • 2016- presidency ends on Juanuary 20, 2017
  • Present- resides in Washington, D.C.
